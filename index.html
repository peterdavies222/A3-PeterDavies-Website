Adaptogens and hemp extract:
Relaxation, reimagined.

With their amazing benefits including stress-relief, health boosts, and improvements to mental clarity, it's no surprise that adaptogens have been used and enjoyed by humans for thousands of years. Why not give them a try?

Hemp extract's benefits don't stop at stress relief. This super ingredient fights skin issues, inflammation, and aging. Who needs alcohol, when you can boost your body whilst having a great time too?

Made by Aussies, for everyone.

Our mission at Nectar has always been simple: to create high quality, tasty, healthy drinks, for anyone looking to have a good time. Why aim for anything less?

A state of the art brewery.

Construction of our brewery began in late 2022, after over two years of planning. Not only are we proud of our products, but also of our epic estate. Come down, check it out, and have a drink or three while you're here!

An enthusiastic team.

Our hard working team are the ones that have brought Nectar to life. Working day and night to brew your favourite Nectar drinks — and develop new flavours — it's not hard to see why our drinks just taste so good.

Natural, locally grown ingredients.

The organic, West Australian grown ingredients are what really set Nectar apart. Not only are our hemp extract and adaptogens extracted from South West produce, but all of our ingredients — from blueberries to basil — are grown right here on our doorstep. One sip and you'll taste the difference.
